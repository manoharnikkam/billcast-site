Privacy Policy

Effective March 2026

Overview

BillCast is developed by Manohar Nikkam. This privacy policy explains how BillCast handles your data. The short version: your data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to any server.

Data Storage

  • All data is stored on-device and synced via iCloud using Apple's infrastructure
  • No data is sent to third-party servers
  • Receipt images (premium feature) are stored in your personal iCloud container
  • Your data syncs automatically across your devices using the same Apple ID

Analytics & Tracking

  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are included in the app
  • No personally identifiable information is collected by the developer
  • We do not use cookies, fingerprinting, or any form of usage tracking

Email Scanning

If you enable the email scanning feature, BillCast processes your emails entirely on-device. Email content is analyzed locally to detect bills and payment confirmations. Your email content never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any external server.

Deleting Your Data

You can delete all of your BillCast data at any time by deleting the app from your device. You can also use the data management options within the app's Settings to selectively remove data. Removing the app will remove all locally stored data. Data stored in your iCloud container can be managed through your iCloud account settings.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please visit our Support page or email us at support@billcast.store.
